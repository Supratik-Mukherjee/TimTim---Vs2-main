Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days Pan India. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for select pin codes.
Do you offer bulk/wholesale pricing?
Yes! Contact us on WhatsApp or email with your requirements and we'll send you our wholesale catalogue.
Can I return a product?
We accept returns within 7 days for unopened items. An unboxing video is required for damage claims. Opened fragrance oils and waxes cannot be returned.
Which wax is best for beginners?
We recommend Soy Wax 464 for beginners — it's forgiving, has great scent throw, and produces clean-burning candles. Check out our Beginner Kit too!
