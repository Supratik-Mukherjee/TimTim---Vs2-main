How it began

A candle maker's dream became a store

Timtim by Aritri was born out of a simple frustration — finding high-quality candle making supplies in India was unnecessarily hard. Long lead times, inconsistent quality, and no guidance for beginners.

So Aritri did what any passionate maker would do — she sourced it herself, tested everything rigorously, and opened her doors to the candle-making community across India.

Today, Timtim by Aritri is trusted by hobbyists, small businesses, and professional candle studios from Mumbai to Meghalaya.
